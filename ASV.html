Anantapur Sports Village




Nurtures talented and motivated athletes to succeed by provision of quality sports training, education, and support in professional and personal development. Currently, 7 programs (5 residential) with 510 children. The ASV has the following facilities:
  • 3 Cricket grounds
  • 400 mts. Athletics Track with 8 lanes
  • 2 Volleyball courts
  • 5 Tennis courts
  • Football ground
  • 2 Hockey grounds
  • Gymnasium
  • 16 dormitories (housing 20 students each)
  • Auditorium
  • Guest Houses
