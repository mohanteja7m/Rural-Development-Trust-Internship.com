Internship Schedule - Atmakuru Group 2023





Six of us— Rajendra Kumar, B.M. Saswati, S.M. Abul Kalam, Pragya Paramita Das, Sneha Das, and Mohan Teja —visited the Atmakuru and Kalyandurg area villages. We evaluated our visits based on the effectiveness and development of the schemes' implementation and made deductions about the data from the field study. We have been assigned the sectors of the RDT based on each person's interest in the sector after reviewing all the visits and gathering the information through focus groups and interviews. This ensures that each sector in the RDT will be covered and explored in detail and methodically so that each sector can draw its conclusions.
