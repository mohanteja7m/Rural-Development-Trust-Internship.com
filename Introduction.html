Rural Development Trust (RDT) Anantapur stands as a vibrant and forward-thinking organization committed to cultivating sustainable development and instigating positive transformations within the Anantapur district. Founded with a clear vision of empowering local communities, RDT Anantapur has played a pivotal role in spearheading a multitude of initiatives geared towards enhancing the socio-economic landscape of the region.

At its core, RDT Anantapur operates with the unwavering objective of fostering sustainable development. The organization recognizes the intricate web of factors that contribute to the overall well-being of a community, encompassing economic, social, and environmental dimensions. Through its multifaceted approach, RDT Anantapur addresses these dimensions to bring about holistic and enduring change. One of the distinctive hallmarks of RDT Anantapur's mission is its active involvement in diverse initiatives. Whether through educational programs, healthcare interventions, or economic empowerment projects, the organization strategically targets key areas that directly impact the lives of the local population. By recognizing the interdependence of these facets, RDT Anantapur endeavors to create a comprehensive support system that uplifts the community at large.

The visionary founders of RDT Anantapur envisioned an organization that transcends traditional charity models. Instead, they aspired to create a dynamic force that collaborates with local communities, working alongside them to identify their needs and co-create solutions. This participatory approach ensures that initiatives are not imposed from external sources but organically emerge from the collective wisdom of the community members themselves. Over the years, RDT Anantapur has evolved into a catalyst for change, channeling its efforts into areas where they can make a meaningful and lasting impact. By championing the cause of empowerment, the organization has become a beacon of hope for countless individuals in the district. Through skill development programs, access to quality education, and healthcare services, RDT Anantapur endeavors to break the cycle of poverty and equip individuals with the tools they need to shape their destinies..

Rural Development Trust (RDT) is registered under the Indian Registration Act, 1908 and 12A of the Income Tax Act.




Organizational Structure of RDT

Senior Core Team:

  • The Board of Trustees has entrusted the responsibility for the implementation of strategies, policies, and decisions pertaining to operational management, including financial oversight, to RDT's Senior Core Team.
  • This team, led by the Executive Director, consists of sector and regional directors. Their role encompasses the effective execution of the directives established by the Board, ensuring the efficient management of RDT's operations.

    • General Core Team:

  • RDT's Core Team holds the responsibility for the initiation and review of programs and projects across the organization.
  • This team is comprised of regional directors, area team leaders overseeing program implementation in 75-100 villages, and is presided over by the Programme Director.
  • Their collective role involves the inception and evaluation of initiatives throughout the organization, ensuring alignment with overarching objectives.
  • The Program Director serves as the chairperson, providing leadership and coordination to foster the effective execution of RDT's programs and projects.

    • Women Core Team:

    The Women's Core Team comprises senior female staff members representing various departments and positions within RDT. The primary objectives of this team include:
  • Providing a platform for women staff members to openly express concerns related to gender issues.
  • Conducting training and capacity-building initiatives. Advocating and influencing area teams to enhance the participation and involvement of women in all program activities.
  • Integrating a gender-sensitive approach into all organizational activities.
  • Addressing individual instances of discrimination. This dedicated team plays a pivotal role in fostering an inclusive and gender-responsive environment within RDT, ensuring the well-being and equitable engagement of women across the organization.
