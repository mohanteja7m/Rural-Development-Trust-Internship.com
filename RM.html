Resource Mobilisation



The overarching goal is to empower Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) to assume a central role in steering their own development journey. This empowerment is facilitated through a concerted effort to raise awareness among PWDs about their legal rights and entitlements. The key thrust is to equip them with the knowledge and tools necessary to access various government benefits. This multifaceted approach encompasses securing government recognition, obtaining essential documents such as medical certificates, and navigating avenues for accessing an array of privileges, including travel concessions, house sites, scholarships, pensions, and various allowances.

Government recognition stands as a pivotal aspect of this empowerment process. Facilitating the acquisition of official recognition for PWDs is crucial, as it serves as a gateway to unlocking a spectrum of benefits and privileges. This involves guiding PWDs through the procedures required to obtain medical certificates, a foundational document that not only validates their disability status but also opens doors to a host of government-mandated provisions.

The initiative extends its scope to facilitate PWDs' access to diverse benefits, ranging from travel concessions to house sites, scholarships, pensions, and other allowances. Travel concessions, for instance, not only enhance the mobility of PWDs but also contribute to their overall inclusivity in societal activities. House sites, scholarships, and pensions become not just entitlements but tools for fostering independence, economic stability, and educational advancement. The initiative recognizes the multifaceted needs of PWDs and endeavors to eliminate barriers that hinder their access to these crucial benefits.
